# FieldNotes — Demo Walkthrough

## For Prospect Calls (5 minutes)

### Minute 0-1: The Hook
> "Mike runs Precision HVAC — 5 techs, 12 commercial accounts. Here's what his dashboard looks like right now."

Show: `dashboard.html?biz=2`
- Point to: 4 stops completed, 1 issue flagged, 6 actions pending
- "He got this without asking anyone a single question."

### Minute 1-2: Show It Live  
> "Let me show you how it actually works. Grab your phone."

Tell them to open Telegram, search **@Field_notesbot_bot**, and send:
> `riverside: all good, replaced 2 filters, client asked about maintenance contract`

Watch the dashboard update in real-time. New log appears. Action created ("maintenance contract follow-up").

> "Notice what James DIDN'T do. No app download. No login. No forms. He opened the messaging app already on his phone. Took 10 seconds."

Switch to summary view:
> "End of day, Mike gets this in his inbox. 4 stops. 1 issue. Supply list. Missed-stop check. He reads it in 60 seconds and knows exactly what happened — and what needs to happen tomorrow."

### Minute 4-5: The Close
> "Mike pays $79/mo for this. Less than one missed follow-up call. Your crew already texts you — this just makes those texts useful."

> "Want to try it with your own accounts? Free for 30 days. I'll set you up in 10 minutes."

**"What's it cost?"**
"$79/mo for a team of 5. Free for 30 days. No credit card. Cancel anytime."

"10 minutes. Tell me your accounts and workers. That's it."
