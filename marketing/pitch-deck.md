## FAQs (for sales calls)

**Q: Does this replace my CRM?**
A: No. FieldNotes complements your existing systems. It handles the "what happened in the field" piece that your CRM doesn't. We're exploring GHL/Housecall Pro integrations for v2.

**Q: What if a worker doesn't have a smartphone?**
A: SMS support is coming (v1.5). For now, Telegram works on any smartphone. If a worker truly can't use it, they can text the owner — who forwards it to the bot.

**Q: What if the AI misunderstands a note?**
A: The original note is always preserved. You can edit the parsed result. The AI gets better over time as it learns your account names and common phrases.

**Q: Is my data secure?**
A: Yes. Data is encrypted at rest and in transit. Workers only see their own notes. Owners see everything.

**Q: Can I cancel anytime?**
A: Yes. No contracts, no cancellation fees. Your data is exportable (CSV/PDF).

**Q: What's the setup process?**
A: 10 minutes. You define your accounts, add your workers' Telegram IDs, and we set up the bot. That's it.
