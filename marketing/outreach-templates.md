## Qualifying Questions (for discovery calls)

1. How many field workers do you have?
2. How do they communicate what happened at each stop?
3. What happens to those texts/messages at the end of the day?
4. How often do issues or follow-ups slip through the cracks?
5. Have you tried any field service software before? What happened?
6. If you could wave a magic wand, what would you want to know at the end of each day?
