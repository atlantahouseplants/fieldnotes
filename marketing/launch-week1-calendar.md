## Rules that keep accounts alive
1. **No links in FB group posts** — say "link in my profile" or DM it. Groups ban link-droppers.
2. **Comment 3x more than you post** in groups. Week 1 is reputation-building; Week 2 is pitching.
3. **Every post ends with one CTA:** "Try it free 30 days — no card: fieldnotesapp.io" or "DM me."
4. **Reply to every comment within an hour** if possible — algorithm + trust.
5. Cross-post same content to all 6 platforms; tweak tone: LinkedIn = founder story, X/Threads = punchy, IG/TikTok = visual, FB = groups + page.
