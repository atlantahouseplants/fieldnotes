## First comment (maker posts this at launch)
Hey Product Hunt 👋

I run a commercial plant service business in Atlanta — 18 accounts, solo operator. For years my "system" was texting myself notes between stops. Supply needs, client requests, issues — all buried in my message history by Friday.

I tried the real field-service software. My crew (me and whoever's helping that week) wouldn't use it. Too many fields, too many taps, another app to remember.

So I built FieldNotes around the one behavior that already worked: **talking between stops.**

Worker sends a voice/text note → AI parses it → matched to the right account → issues, supplies, and follow-ups become action items → owner gets a Telegram summary every evening.

It's how I run my own business today — the daily summary in the screenshots is my real route.

Beta is open: 30-day free trial, no credit card. First 10 customers lock beta pricing for life.

I'd love feedback from anyone who's run a crew — especially the "my guys won't use software" crowd. That's exactly who this is for.
