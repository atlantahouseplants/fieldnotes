Frequently asked questions

Do my workers need to install an app?
No. They use Telegram — the messaging app already on their phone. Zero training, zero installs. (SMS coming on Crew.)
What is "Ask FieldNotes"?
Your crew asks the bot anything — gate codes, access notes, when a client was last serviced, open issues — and it answers from YOUR accounts and service history. The product gets smarter the more you use it.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. No contracts, no cancellation fees. You own your data — export anytime.
How does the free trial work?
30 days, full access, no credit card required. At the end of the trial, choose a plan or your account pauses — no charges.
